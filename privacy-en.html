Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ehabit! We understand the importance of privacy to you, so we've created this privacy policy to detail how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Our commitment: Your data belongs to you. We will not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Collect

Ehabit primarily collects the following types of information:

  • Habit Data: Habit names, goals, completion records, etc.
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature usage frequency, and other anonymous statistical data
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as device model and operating system version
  • iCloud Data: If you enable sync, data will be stored in your iCloud account

2.2 Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Sensitive data from other apps

3. Information Usage

The information we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving app functionality
  • Syncing your data to other devices
  • Sending reminder notifications
  • Analyzing app performance and usage (anonymously)
  • Providing customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms.

4.2 iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync functionality:

  • Data will be encrypted and stored in your iCloud account
  • Only the Apple ID you're signed in with can access this data
  • We cannot access data stored in your iCloud

4.3 Security Measures

  • Use industry-standard encryption to protect data transmission
  • Regularly update security measures
  • Limit employee access to data

5. Third-Party Services

Ehabit may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud: For data synchronization (if enabled)
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Apple Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics (optional)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the relevant terms.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: View information we have collected about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Ehabit is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • App update descriptions

Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us
