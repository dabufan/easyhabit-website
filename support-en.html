Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Easyhabit?
After downloading the app, tap the "+" button to add your first habit. Set the habit name, target frequency, and reminder time, then start recording your progress.
Will my data sync to other devices?
Yes, Easyhabit supports iCloud sync. Make sure your devices are signed in with the same Apple ID and enable iCloud sync in settings.
How do I set up reminder notifications?
You can set reminder times when adding or editing habits. You can also manage all notification permissions in settings to ensure the app has permission to send notifications.
How do I use widgets?
Long press on an empty area of your home screen, tap the "+" button in the top left, search for "Easyhabit" and add a widget. You can choose different widget sizes to quickly view and record habits.
How do I backup my data?
Through iCloud sync, your data is automatically backed up to the cloud. You can also export data in settings or use your device's iCloud backup feature.

Troubleshooting

App won't launch

If the app won't start properly, try these solutions:

  • Restart your device
  • Check if your device has sufficient storage space
  • Ensure your iOS version meets app requirements
  • Re-download the app from the App Store

Data sync issues

If you encounter data sync problems:

  • Check if your network connection is working
  • Confirm you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
  • Check iCloud storage space in settings
  • Try turning iCloud sync off and on again

Notifications not working

If you're not receiving reminder notifications:

  • Check your device's notification permission settings
  • Make sure Do Not Disturb mode is not enabled
  • Check notification settings within the app
  • Restart your device to refresh notification services

Widget display issues

If widgets are displaying incorrectly:

  • Remove and re-add the widget
  • Restart your device
  • Check if the app is running normally
  • Update to the latest version of the app
